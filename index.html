The

The Meg is a 2018 science fiction action horror film directed by Jon Turteltaub. The film stars Jason Statham as a deep-sea rescue diver who must confront a giant prehistoric shark known as the Megalodon, which has been awakened and is threatening the safety of the ocean and those in it. With a star-studded cast and high-stakes action, The Meg is a thrilling adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat.
